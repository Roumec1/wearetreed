FAQ

Questions you might be having right now

What types of corporate gifts do you offer?
We offer a wide range of eco-friendly gifts made from natural materials. Wooden wireless chargers, ergonomic laptop stands, desk organizers, wooden car fragrances. Plus gift boxes combining products from our workshop and other local makers.
Are all products made from sustainable materials?
We make all our products with sustainability in mind. We use local wood, eco-friendly finishes and recyclable packaging.
Can we see samples before ordering?
Yes. You can order a sample before placing the final order, or even several variants / iterations. Contact us for details.
What's the minimum order quantity?
The minimum order depends on the specific product and the level of customization. The standard is from 10 pieces with engraving.
How long does production of a custom order take?
Usually 1–4 weeks, depending on the complexity of customization and order size.
Do you perform quality control before production starts?
Every design goes through an approval step, and products are checked before shipping.
How do you ensure consistent quality on large orders?
We combine modern production technology with manual quality checks at every step.
Do you offer a warranty?
We offer a standard 2-year warranty on all our products.
What's the typical delivery time for large orders?
Standard 2–8 weeks including production, depending on order volume.
Do you offer express production or shipping?
Contact us for urgent orders and we'll arrange individual options.
Can a delivery be split across multiple locations?
We can deliver products directly to your clients at multiple addresses on request.
Do you provide shipment tracking?
Yes. We'll send you a tracking number after the order ships.
Can we add our company logo to the products?
Yes. We offer several branding options: laser engraving, custom packaging including graphic design, or producing a product that directly reflects your company logo.
Do you offer fully custom product manufacturing?
If you have a specific product idea, we're happy to walk through the options with you and design a one-of-a-kind solution.
Can we see a digital preview before ordering?
Once the design is approved we'll send you a visualization, and in some cases a physical prototype.
How do you keep your production sustainable?
We use local wood, eco-friendly finishes and packaging. Our production is built around minimizing waste.
Where are your products made?
We make all products by hand in Šumperk, Czechia.
Do you offer eco-friendly packaging?
All our products are packaged by default in recyclable or biodegradable materials.
